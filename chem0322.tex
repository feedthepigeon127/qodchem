Sodium ethanoate, \ch{CH3COONa}, is the active ingredient in reusable heating pads and it can be prepared from vinegar. Which of the following substances can be mixed with vinegar to obtain \ch{CH3COONa}? 

\begin{itemize}
\item[(1)] baking soda (\ch{NaHCO3})
\item[(2)] caustic soda (\ch{NaOH})
\item[(3)] washing soda (\ch{Na2CO3})
\end{itemize}

\begin{choices}
\choice (1) and (2) only
\choice (1) and (3) only
\choice (2) and (3) only
\choice (1), (2) and (3)
\end{choices}

\begin{answer}
\hrulefill\par
\textbf{Ans: D}

\end{answer}
