Airbags in automobiles work by releasing gases in airbags within ten of milliseconds after the crash. One chemical that can be used for such purpose is an ionic compound consisting of sodium and nitrogen:
\begin{center}
\ch{Na_xN_y{\sld} ->[heat] Na{\sld} + N_2{\gas}}
\end{center}
Suppose \SI{16.4}{\gram} of sodium is obtained and \SI{25.7}{\cubic\dm} of nitrogen gas is released upon complete decomposition of the ionic compound. What is the mole ratio of \ch{Na} to \ch{N} in the ionic compound (x : y)?

(Relative atomic masses: $\ch{Na}=23.0$, $\ch{N}=14.0$, molar volume $=\SI{24}{\cubic\dm}$)

\begin{choices}
\choice $1:3$
\choice $2:3$
\choice $3:1$
\choice $3:2$
\end{choices}
